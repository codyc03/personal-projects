About Us

Nestled at the base of the majestic Wasatch Mountains in Draper, Utah, we’re a small but passionate team turning the art of firearm customization into a uniquely personal experience. At LeBaron Arms, we believe every firearm has a story, and our mission is to help you tell yours — one engraved masterpiece at a time.

Our journey started with a simple idea: firearms can be more than just tools or collectibles; they can be an extension of who you are. Whether it’s a cherished heirloom passed down through generations, a commemorative piece to celebrate a milestone, or a trusted companion for your outdoor adventures, we see each project as an opportunity to blend functionality with artistry.

Armed (pun intended) with precision tools, steady hands, and a love for all things creative, we specialize in custom engraving that transforms firearms into one-of-a-kind works of art. From intricate scrollwork to bold, statement-making designs, we work closely with our customers to bring their vision to life. Our small team takes pride in every detail, obsessing over every curve, line, and flourish to ensure that your firearm is as unique as you are.

Being based in Draper isn’t just about enjoying the stunning scenery — although we do think the mountain views add a little extra inspiration to our craft. It’s also about community. We’re a company born out of a love for people, stories, and the shared pride of creating something truly special. You won’t find any corporate red tape here— just a group of folks who love what they do and care deeply about doing it well.

When you work with us, you’re not just another customer; you’re part of the family. Whether you’re dropping by our shop to brainstorm a design, asking for advice on which firearm to engrave, or just swinging in to say hello, we’re always thrilled to connect with fellow enthusiasts. Don’t be surprised if you find yourself staying longer than planned—around here, conversations often wander from engraving techniques to swapping hunting stories or sharing the best local hiking spots.

At LeBaron Arms, we’re not just creating custom firearms; we’re creating memories, preserving legacies, and crafting connections. We love what we do, and we’re honored to share our passion with you. Whether you’re looking for something subtle or a design that screams "wow," we’re here to make it happen — with a little bit of Utah charm and a whole lot of heart.

So, here’s to great stories, beautiful craftsmanship, and firearms that truly stand apart. Let’s create something amazing together. Welcome to the LeBaron Arms family!
